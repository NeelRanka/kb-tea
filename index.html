Building a Legacy

The Standard of Excellence

Established in 1995, we are a leading name in Pune's tea industry, built on a legacy of reliability, consistency, and heritage. As a trusted professional wholesale partner, we bridge the gap between India's finest tea gardens and your daily cup.

We are proud owners of the popular tea brands Jay Anand and Tina, known for their superior aroma and rich flavor. In addition to our branded products, we are major suppliers of high-quality loose tea powder, catering to retailers, cafes, and large-scale distribution networks across Maharashtra.
